### 1. Single-cell Metabolomics
Focusing on mass spectrometry-based metabolomics analysis for trace samples and single cells, enabling high-coverage metabolite profiling and discovery of hidden cellular heterogeneity.

### 2. AI in Omic Studies
Constructing prediction models for drug efficacy, pharmaceutical quality discrimination, and food safety risk assessment through the integration of multi-omics data and artificial intelligence.

### 3. Aging-related Diseases
Investigating the underlying mechanisms of aging-related diseases to develop potential therapeutic interventions based on metabolic pathways and biomarker discovery.
